The question of the role of the state has been keenly debated when the US gained its independence; the philosophy of two of the framers of the Constitution, Jefferson and Hamilton, gave rise to two schools of thoughts. The Jeffersonians felt that “he governs best who governs least” while the Hamiltonians favored active government involvement.
